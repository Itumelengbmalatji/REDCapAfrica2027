About the Symposium

The REDCap Africa Symposium brings together researchers, data managers and technologists to foster collaboration, share expertise, and improve data quality for health research across the continent.

Since its inception, the symposium has focused on hands-on training, peer-to-peer learning and building partnerships that strengthen research data systems in Africa.

Our Mission

  • Capacity Building - Empowering institutions with skills to manage health data effectively.
  • Innovation - Showcasing cutting-edge REDCap applications and integrations.
  • Collaboration - Creating networks that strengthen research across Africa.
  • Governance - Promoting ethical and secure data practices

Organizing Committee

The symposium is led by a dedicated committee from universities and research institutions across South Africa and the region.
