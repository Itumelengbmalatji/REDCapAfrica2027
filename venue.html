Venue & Travel

Join us in beautiful Cape Town for the REDCap Africa Symposium 2026 at the Lagoon Beach Hotel & Spa.

Accommodation

Attendees requiring accommodation at Lagoon Beach Hotel & Spa at the conference block rate should email the hotel events department to have their booking linked to the symposium rate.

Email: events@lagoonbeachhotel.co.za

Travel Information

Flights: Fly into Cape Town International Airport (CPT), which offers regular domestic and international routes.

Transport: Ride-hailing services such as Uber and Bolt are available, along with metered taxis and airport shuttle options.

Visa: To request an invitation letter, use the symposium visa form.
