Past Years

A look back at the growth of the REDCap Africa Symposium and the community it brings together.

2025: Durban, South Africa

Hosted by Africa Health Research Institute, the 2025 symposium welcomed 84 participants from 8 countries and 25 institutions. Key highlights included a keynote from REDCap Lead Developer Rob Taylor and hands-on workshops for over 60 delegates.

2024: Johannesburg, South Africa

Hosted by Wits University, the 2024 symposium featured keynote speaker Dr Paul Harris and brought together 106 attendees from 6 countries and 13 institutions, helping strengthen the REDCap community across Africa.
