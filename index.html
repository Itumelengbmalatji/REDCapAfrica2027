About the Symposium

The REDCap Africa Symposium is a premier continental platform for advancing research data management and collaboration. Now in its 10th edition, the symposium convenes a diverse community of researchers, data professionals, and institutional leaders to exchange knowledge, showcase innovations, and strengthen the use of REDCap across Africa.

Through technical sessions, peer learning, and networking opportunities, the symposium supports institutions to enhance data quality, improve research efficiency, and drive impactful evidence generation.

Why attend

  • Learn REDCap best practices from African practitioners
  • Connect with peers from research, healthcare and tech
  • Discover governance, ethics and secure data management
  • Access practical training for beginners and advanced users
